Woya

Long ago, two kindred spirits were torn apart by an unimaginable darkness, banished to the ends of time...

Now, they find themselves facing one another once more, this time as mortal enemies...





Within the deep trench of Woya lie mysterious caverns which emit an amaranthine light... What powers might they hold?

Tip: Caverns are related and destroy special artifacts found within sister caves upon entrance.

Use the W, A, S and D keys to move, and the ENTER key to lock in your decison.

Use RMB to fire and SPACEBAR to jump.


Game must be played in full screen, on Google Chrome for full experience.
